About Us

Fal-Mal Hope Initiative (FAMHI) is a national independent humanitarian non-profit organization registered under Part C of the Companies and Allied Matters Act 2004. We are committed to empowering vulnerable communities through sustainable solutions in WASH, shelter, protection, education, livelihoods, and durable solutions for displaced persons.

Our Core Competencies

Protection

We focus on protecting the most vulnerable, ensuring access to legal aid Assistance (on Legal & Identity Rights, Housing, Land & Property Rights), psychosocial support, and safeguarding.

More →
WASH

Provision of Water, Sanitation and Hygiene (WASH) We deliver essential water, sanitation, and hygiene solutions, including water supply systems, sanitation infrastructure, and hygiene.

More →
Agriculture & Livelihoods

We support communities in achieving sustainable livelihoods by providing agricultural training, promoting climate-smart farming techniques, and facilitating access to essential tools

More →
Education

We work to increase access to quality education, building schools, training teachers, and promoting educational outreach for children and communities with equity, innovation, and empowerment.

More →
Shelter

Our shelter programs provide safe, dignified housing solutions for displaced populations, offering emergency shelter and long-term housing solutions to those affected by crises & natural disaster.

More →
Camp Coordination

We coordinate camp management, train committees, and ensure fair, safe access to essential services for displaced persons. We promote dignity, safety, inclusion, and community empowerment.
