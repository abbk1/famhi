Our Past Performances

A detailed look at how we have impacted communities

Education

We have supported over 1,200 children in accessing quality education through school construction, teacher training, and the distribution of learning materials. Our programs focus on ensuring every child has equal opportunities to learn and thrive.

Food Security

We distributed food and nutrition support to more than 2,000 families, ensuring that vulnerable households had access to safe and nutritious meals. Our interventions reduce hunger and support community resilience.

WASH (Water, Sanitation & Hygiene)

We improved access to clean water and sanitation facilities for 950 individuals, promoting hygiene awareness and reducing the risk of waterborne diseases in IDP camps and host communities.

Shelter

We provided emergency shelter to over 600 families, offering safety, dignity, and protection for displaced populations. Our shelter solutions help restore stability and security during crises.

Protection

We identified and referred 400 protection cases, including GBV and child protection, to relevant partners for specialized services. Our protection efforts ensure vulnerable groups receive the care and justice they deserve.
